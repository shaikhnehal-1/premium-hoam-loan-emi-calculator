📌 What is a Home Loan EMI Calculator?

A Home Loan EMI Calculator helps you calculate your monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

It is an essential financial tool for planning your home purchase and understanding your repayment structure.

🧮 EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Loan Tenure (months)
